Isaac Asimov

An astounding writing career

A Timeline of Dr. Asimov's life:

  • 1920 Born in Petrovichi, Russia.
  • 1923 Family emigrates to the United States.
  • 1925 Teaches himself to read.
  • 1925 Enrolled in first grade.
  • 1931 Begins writing as a hobby.
  • 1939 First story, "Marooned Off Vesta," published in Amazing Stories
  • 1941 "Nightfall" published.
  • 1941 "Foundation" series begun.
  • 1942-45 Works at the Navy Yard during WWII.
  • 1946 Drafted into the US Army.
  • 1948 Earns PhD in Biochemistry, Columbia.
  • 1950 First book, "Pebble in the Sky" published.
  • 1958 Becomes full-time writer.
  • 1969 Publishes 100th book.
  • 1979 Publishes 200th book.
  • 1984 Publishes 300th book.
  • 1988 Publishes 400th book.
  • 1992 Publishes 500th book.
  • 1992 Dies of complications of HIV due to a 1983 blood tranfusion.
  • 2003 513th and last book published.
  • Since 1992 Awards, a lecture series, a martian crater and an asteriod have been named after Dr. Asimov
